# 🔥 Phoenix Tracker

**An OS for serious aspirants.**

A free, single-file study tracker built for BCS and government bank job aspirants in Bangladesh. No installs, no accounts, no ads — just open it and start tracking.

**[👉 Use it now (no download needed)](https://kowsar0x9.github.io/Phoenix-Tracker/)**

---

## Why this exists

Most study trackers are either too generic (built for any student, fit for none) or too complex (require signup, sync, subscriptions). Phoenix Tracker was built by an aspirant, for aspirants — around the actual rhythm of long-haul competitive exam prep: months-long phases, mock score trends, and the daily grind of showing up.

## Features

- **Auto-calculated Month/Week** — tracks real elapsed weeks from your fixed start date, not calendar months
- **Mock score tracking** — log, edit, and delete scores over time (decimals supported)
- **Daily Log with Study | Other toggle** — track study sessions *and* life outside study (gym, family, rest, errands) without mixing the two
- **Dashboard with Study / Other / Combined views** — a 24-hour breakdown plus an honest, non-judgmental "lost day" verdict based on a threshold you set yourself
- **Custom activity categories** — make the "Other" tracker fit your own life
- **History tab** — full log of past days, including non-study days
- **Daily motivational quotes** — a rotating pool of 20, in English and Bangla
- **100% offline & private** — everything is stored in your browser's local storage. Nothing is ever sent to a server. No login, no tracking, no data leaves your device.

## How to use it

**Option 1 — Instant (recommended):**
Open the [live link](https://kowsar0x9.github.io/Phoenix-Tracker/) in any browser (phone or desktop). That's it.

**Option 2 — Download and run locally:**
1. Download `index.html` from this repo
2. Double-click it, or open it in any browser
3. Bookmark it — your data stays saved in that browser going forward

## 🇬🇧 English

### Where is my data stored?

Phoenix Tracker stores all your data **only inside your current browser's Local Storage.**

It does **NOT** use cloud storage.

Your data may be lost if you:

- Clear browser data or cache
- Reinstall your browser
- Switch to another browser
- Open the tracker on another phone or computer
- Factory reset your device

### ✅ Stay Safe

- Regularly **Export a Backup** from Settings.
- Store the backup file somewhere safe (Google Drive, OneDrive, USB, etc.).
- Import the backup whenever you move to another device.

> **Remember:** Browser Storage ≠ Cloud Storage.
